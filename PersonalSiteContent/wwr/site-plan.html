Overview

Purpose

My main purpose to this web site is to help bring in new customers.It will help to make it easier for customers to set up appointments Vanuatu Adventours. The website should have our business hours and times of operating months. It should contain prices and deal. It should also have our contact informations. It needs to have a forum where previous customers and inquiring customers can answer and share questions with one another. We will also have a place on the website with pictures.

Audience

My target audience is mostly travelers. I want to provide a place for travelers to come and make memories. I hope to help every travelers who are looking for more adventure in their life, some fulfilment from feeling something new and exciting. I expect the audience to normally view my website through Personal Computers, Laptop, IPad, Tablet, and Phone and through Facebook, sharing and other social media adds.
